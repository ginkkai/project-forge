## Case 001

E## Case 001

Equipment:

CNC-001


Date:

2025-03-12


Symptom:

During continuous machining, spindle temperature increases abnormally and machine stops automatically.


Error Code:

E102


Root Cause:

Insufficient cooling system performance caused by blocked coolant filter.


Troubleshooting Steps:

1. Check coolant level.
2. Check coolant pump pressure.
3. Inspect coolant filter condition.
4. Clean or replace blocked filter.


Solution:

Cleaned coolant filter and restored normal coolant circulation.


Verification Result:

Machine operated continuously for 2 hours without temperature alarm.

## Case 002

Equipment:

CNC-001


Date:

2025-04-08


Symptom:

During machining operation, coolant flow becomes weak and cutting temperature increases.


Error Code:

E205


Root Cause:

Coolant pump pressure decreased due to pump wear and insufficient coolant level.


Troubleshooting Steps:

1. Check coolant tank level.
2. Check coolant pump operation status.
3. Measure coolant pressure.
4. Inspect pump condition.


Solution:

Refilled coolant and replaced worn coolant pump.


Verification Result:

Coolant pressure returned to normal and machining process completed successfully.

## Case 003

Equipment:

CNC-001


Date:

2025-05-21


Symptom:

Machined parts show dimensional deviation and poor surface finish.


Error Code:

T301


Root Cause:

Cutting tool exceeded recommended service life and became worn.


Troubleshooting Steps:

1. Check tool usage time.
2. Inspect tool edge condition.
3. Compare machining parameters with standard settings.
4. Replace damaged tool.


Solution:

Replaced cutting tool and recalibrated machining parameters.


Verification Result:

Product dimensions returned to specification and surface quality improved.
