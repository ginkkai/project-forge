# User Research

# 用户研究


## Target Users

目标用户：

The Industrial AI Knowledge Assistant focuses on three primary user groups.

工业AI知识助手主要服务三个用户群体。


---

# User 1: Overseas Dealer Technical Staff

## 用户1：海外代理商技术人员


## Background

Technical employees at overseas dealers support customers with equipment operation and maintenance.

海外代理商技术人员负责客户设备使用和维护支持。


## Scenario

A customer reports an equipment problem.

The technician needs to quickly find:

- Operation information
- Maintenance procedures
- Troubleshooting solutions

客户反馈设备问题。

技术人员需要快速找到：

- 操作信息
- 维修流程
- 故障解决方案


## Pain Points

1. Information is difficult to search.

资料难以快速搜索。


2. Different products have different documentation.

不同产品资料复杂。


3. Waiting for headquarters support takes time.

等待总部支持耗费时间。


---

# User 2: Field Service Engineers

## 用户2：现场维修工程师


## Background

Service engineers solve equipment problems at customer sites.

现场维修工程师需要在客户现场解决设备问题。


## Scenario

A machine stops working.

The engineer needs immediate technical guidance.

设备出现故障。

工程师需要立即获得技术指导。


## Pain Points

1. Depends heavily on personal experience.

高度依赖个人经验。


2. Experienced engineers cannot support every case.

资深工程师无法覆盖所有问题。


3. Knowledge is not efficiently transferred.

经验无法有效传递。


---

# User 3: Training Staff

## 用户3：培训人员


## Background

Training teams teach dealers and new employees.

培训团队负责代理商和新员工培训。


## Pain Points

1. Training materials are scattered.

培训资料分散。


2. Updating knowledge is difficult.

知识更新困难。


3. Different regions have different knowledge levels.

不同地区知识水平不一致。
