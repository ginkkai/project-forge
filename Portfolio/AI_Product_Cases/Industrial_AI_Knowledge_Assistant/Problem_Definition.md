# Problem Definition

## Product Name

Industrial AI Knowledge Assistant

工业AI知识助手


## Background

Engineering machinery companies generate large amounts of technical knowledge:

- Operation manuals
- Maintenance documents
- Service bulletins
- Training materials
- Troubleshooting guides

工程机械企业拥有大量技术知识：

- 操作手册
- 维修资料
- 服务公告
- 培训材料
- 故障排查经验


## Problem

When overseas dealers, technicians, or customers face equipment problems, finding the right information quickly is difficult.

海外代理商、维修人员和客户遇到设备问题时，很难快速找到正确的信息。


## Current Challenges

### 1. Information is scattered

Technical knowledge exists in different formats and locations.

技术资料分散在不同文件和系统中。


### 2. Repeated questions consume expert time

Many common problems still require experienced engineers.

大量重复问题依赖资深工程师。


### 3. Knowledge transfer is inefficient

Experience is difficult to transfer across regions.

跨区域知识传递效率低。


## Opportunity

Use AI to transform industrial knowledge into an intelligent assistant.

利用AI将工业知识转化为智能助手。


The assistant can help users:

- Find information faster
- Solve problems efficiently
- Learn equipment knowledge

帮助用户：

- 快速查找资料
- 提高问题解决效率
- 学习设备知识
