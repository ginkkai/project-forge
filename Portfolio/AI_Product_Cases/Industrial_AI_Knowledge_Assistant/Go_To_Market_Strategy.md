# 3. Value Proposition

## 产品价值主张


Industrial AI Knowledge Assistant helps service teams reduce troubleshooting time by transforming engineering knowledge into actionable AI guidance.


通过AI将工业知识转化为可执行的故障解决方案，帮助服务团队降低维修时间。
