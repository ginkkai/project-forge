# Industrial AI Knowledge Assistant

## AI-powered troubleshooting assistant for industrial service teams


---

# 1. Project Overview

Industrial AI Knowledge Assistant is an AI-powered product designed to help industrial service engineers quickly access technical knowledge and solve equipment problems.

The product transforms engineering documents, maintenance cases, and expert knowledge into an intelligent troubleshooting assistant.


中文：

工业AI知识助手是一款面向工业服务团队的AI产品，通过企业知识库和AI技术帮助工程师快速定位设备问题，提高维修效率。



---

# 2. Problem Definition

Industrial companies have accumulated large amounts of technical knowledge:

- Service manuals
- Maintenance records
- Expert experience
- Troubleshooting cases


However, this knowledge is often:

- Difficult to search
- Scattered across different systems
- Hard for new engineers to access


Result:

Engineers spend significant time finding solutions instead of solving problems.



---

# 3. Target Users


Primary users:

## Field Service Engineers

They need:

- Fast troubleshooting support
- Reliable technical information
- Practical repair guidance


Secondary users:

- Service managers
- Technical trainers
- Engineering experts

# 4. Solution


The product provides an AI troubleshooting assistant based on industrial knowledge.


Core workflow:


User Question

↓

AI Understanding

↓

Knowledge Retrieval

↓

Industrial Case Matching

↓

AI Diagnosis

↓

Solution Recommendation



The AI does not replace engineers.

It helps engineers make faster and better decisions.
